Our Journey

The Rustic Garden Story

🌳

From Seedling to Cup

The Rustic Garden began with a simple belief: coffee should be traceable, transparent, and fair. Every batch carries a code from the moment a seedling is purchased through to the cup in your hands.

Located at Kavutiri, Embu, our farm is more than just a coffee operation. It's a working demonstration of responsible production—where sylvotherapy, aquaculture, and sustainable coffee growing share the same ground.

We support a network of smallholder farmers trained in composting, vegetative propagation, and best practices. Our arrowroot variety, bred on-site, is shared freely with the broader farming community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh is this coffee?

We roast to order. Your coffee is roasted within 24 hours of your order and shipped immediately. Maximum 2 weeks from roasting.

What's the best brewing method?

Light roast works best with pour-over or specialty brewers. Dark roast shines in espresso and French press. All are excellent in automatic drip coffee.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within Kenya. Inquire about international orders—we may be able to arrange custom shipments.

How long does coffee stay fresh?

Best consumed within 3-4 weeks of roasting. Store in an airtight container away from light and heat. Whole beans stay fresher longer than ground.

What does "honey processed" mean?

The cherry fruit is removed, leaving the sticky fruit pulp (honey) to ferment and sun-dry with the bean. This creates naturally sweet, complex flavor.

Can I visit the garden?

Yes! Inquire about sylvotherapy walks, pool visits, and farm tours. Email hello@rusticgardenkenya.org for availability and pricing.
