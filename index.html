Traceability

From seedling to cup — every step is coded.

The Rustic Garden's farm-to-fork model runs on accountability. Each batch carries a code from the moment a seedling is planted through to the cup in your hands, so the origin of what you're drinking is never a question.

01

Seedling

Sourced or vegetatively propagated on-site, coded at the point of purchase.

02

03

04

Cherries hand-picked at peak ripeness; each lot carries its own code.

Beyond the cup

A haven for nature, wellness and sustainable food.

Located at Kavutiri, Embu, the Rustic Garden is where sylvotherapy, aquaculture and coffee growing share the same ground. A working demonstration that responsible production and genuine wellbeing can come from the same acre.

Coffee cherries grow a short walk from where they're honey-processed — the same soil that also breeds our own arrowroot variety.

  • Guided sylvotherapy walks through tree-lined garden paths
  • Chlorine-free swimming pool, gentle on joints and skin
  • Tilapia and catfish ponds, raised with sustainable feed practices
  • Farmer network trained in composting and vegetative propagation
Why it matters

Every bag supports Kenya's seed systems.

Rustic Garden's farm-to-fork model links what's in your cup back to the smallholder farmers, nurseries and seed breeders who made it possible — part of a wider push for traceable, climate-resilient agriculture and food security across Kenya's growing regions.
