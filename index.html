Returns & Exchanges

We stand behind every product we grow and ship from the Rustic Garden. Here's our return policy for Kenya.

Return Policy

We accept returns for defective products only. This includes:

  • Damaged packaging during shipping
  • Products that do not meet our quality standards
  • Mold, moisture, or contamination upon arrival
  • Incorrect items delivered

What to Do

To initiate a return for a defective product:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide photos of the defective item and packaging
  • Include your order number and details
  • We'll arrange a replacement or refund

Exchanges

Unfortunately, we do not accept exchanges for different roast levels, varieties, or product preferences. Each roast is made to order to ensure freshness.

How We Verify

We review all return requests carefully to ensure they meet our policy guidelines. This process typically takes 3–10 business days. Your return is important to us — we verify authenticity to protect both you and our community of farmers.

Quality Assurance

We trace every product from seedling to cup. If a product fails our standards or arrives damaged, we make it right. Your satisfaction and the reputation of the Rustic Garden depend on it.
